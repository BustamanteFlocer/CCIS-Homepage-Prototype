General Orientation on Limited Face-to-Face Classes: Update

Due to unforeseen conflict in schedule and unavailability of some admin heads, the General Orientation for Limited Face-to-Face Classes has been moved. The rescheduled date is on January 31, 2022 at 1:00 PM.
In the meantime, you may fill out this google form: bit.ly/3Asi2lk for your pre-existing concerns and queries. So that they will be catered or entertained during the live orientation.
The USG understands the possible frustration that this movement may bring. However, this will also serve as an avenue for the organizers and other people involved ample time for preparation to fully reach the objective of this event.
Please be guided.

Apply now for a Full Time Instructor!

Status: Contract of Service (COS)
Basic Salary: Php 31, 262.00/month
 
Qualifications:
1. Must be a graduate of any ITE Programs (BSCS/BSIS/BSIT) or other ITE-allied disciplines.
2. Preferably holds at least a masters degree or units in ITE Graduate Programs/ITE-allied programs.
3. Preferably with teaching experience. Fresh grads are also encourage to apply!
4. Willing to teach ITE courses and perform other duties and task assigned.
5. Flexible and can handle time-sensitive deliverables.
